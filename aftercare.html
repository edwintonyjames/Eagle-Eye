TATTO AFTER CARE

Tatto after care plays a very important role when it comes to good quality healing of tattoo

Creating professional tattoo design

FOLLOW THESE STEPS



Remove Covering Within One hour

Wash Tatto with Anti Bacterial Soap

Apply a light layer of ointment

Repeat Steps 2&3 Times, 3 Times daily for 15 days

No Sun&Soaking underwater for 15 days

Use Lotion & Sunblock to preserve your Tattoo

Do not scratch or pick your Tatto skin while healing

Listen to your Tatto artist, not your friend!
